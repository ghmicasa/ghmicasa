The game of selling numbers. It was so hilarious, and I kept laughing, I couldn’t control the joy of reflecting on things which plays with the human psychology.

He said, “So, what’s next? Phone with five cameras? ,” and I laughed so hard. 

It all started with our discussion on how numbers, when the count increases, affects human psychology. A few years back we saw a new entrant in mobile technology. The company was new to market, and had to compete with the older players. The new entrant came with a genius idea of selling numbers rather than technology and started marketing its product with numbers, and it worked. Phone with multiple number of cameras, we all have seen how everyone is crazy about numbers. Everyone running for the phone with multiple camera is new norm/style, and as the number increases, our desire/lust increases. Those days are gone when technology was about bringing all functions in one camera, and the art and research of producing something beautiful. Now, only numbers sell.

The new entrant then is a giant mobile manufacturing company now. The old players are now catching up with the similar idea. They are also selling numbers now. Don’t know where it will stop, may be when there is no space left at the back panel it will stop. 

The way it all is advertised, it’s so hilarious. Although, nothing wrong with it, that’s the way things work, and everyone fell for the genius idea/game.
